Overview

Our outbound workflow had become a stumbling block. As a relationship-driven executive search firm, cold outreach was our lifeblood and how we built trust. However, our stack (PhoneBurner, Mailchimp, and a duct-taped Salesforce sync) added friction at every step. Reps avoided it, follow-up slipped, and we lost visibility into what was working.

I had built that original stack myself, using PhoneBurner for dialing and Mailchimp for basic sequencing, because it was the fastest and cheapest way to get something live. It gave us a working model for cadence logic and helped prove that automated follow-up could move the needle. While it worked in the beginning and helped reps get used to structured follow-up, the cracks widened as the team grew.

The sync only ran once a day, which meant the data was always a step behind. Admin-only backend controls slowed down fixes, and the picklist-heavy Salesforce UI frustrated reps. They stopped using the system, and we lost visibility into what was working.
