System Adoption Highlights
Outreach rollout led to full adoption across all outbound roles within 180 days.

Reps abandoned prior DIY tools (PhoneBurner, Mailchimp) without pushback. Cadences were used daily, call volume increased by 57%, and email follow-ups jumped more than 300%.

New compensation model replaced flat commission with KPI-weighted payouts.

Roles were paid based on funnel-stage influence, not just closed revenue. Comp risk decreased, morale improved, and high-performing top-of-funnel roles stopped churning.

Forecasting was no longer a spreadsheet scramble.

Leadership relied on KPI-based dashboards and rep-level activity data to validate pipeline. Meetings shifted from anecdote to input/output clarity.

CRM ownership logic ended deal collision and status ambiguity.

Each contact and lead had explicit routing rules tied to persona, funnel stage, and owner role. Internal conflict dropped, and time spent resolving duplicate outreach went to zero.

Email validation system reduced bounce rate by 32% in the first 60 days.

Manual checks, validation APIs, and enrichment tools were integrated into a clean Salesforce workflow. SDRs spent less time chasing bad data and more time booking connects.

Comp forecasting became immediate and transparent.

Reps saw projected bonuses update automatically from their logged activity; no finance back-and-forth, no spreadsheets, no guesswork.

Dashboards became coaching tools, not just executive scorecards.

Role-specific KPIs were visible, trackable, and tied to payout structure. New hires ramped faster and managers had real-time visibility into behavior, not just results.
